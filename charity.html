Charity


Anchors was recorded with the idea that music is best served when it's helping others - whether it be emotionally or, in this case, financially.


By donating to download Anchors, you'll be helping to fund music programs in high poverty areas around the country where programs are often under funded or cut completely. All donations will be made through Donors Choose, which is an organization that allows teachers to request funds for materials for their students.


As of April 2014, we've helped over 1,500 students in 15 classrooms, with over $3,600 donated


I hope you enjoy listening to Anchors as much as I enjoyed making it.
